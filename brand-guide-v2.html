09 — Guidelines Tobi Oredein

Do's & Don'ts

protecting the brand voice

Fresh, optimistic, and confidently bold — but never garish, never corporate, never cold.

Do

  • Lead with butter yellow

    It's the signature — optimistic, unexpected, memorable

  • Use bolder font weights

    Medium/500 for elegance, Black/900 for stats

  • Mix yellow + pink thoughtfully

    They complement each other beautifully

  • Keep backgrounds light

    Silver and soft tones, dark only for dramatic stats

  • Feel fresh and optimistic

    Energy without being overwhelming

Don't

  • Make it garish

    Balance yellow with silver and neutrals

  • Go too light on fonts

    This palette needs confident weight

  • Use corporate aesthetics

    No Helvetica, no navy, no stock photos

  • Overdo the cherry red

    It's an accent, not a primary color

  • Lose the warmth

    This palette is about joy and confidence
