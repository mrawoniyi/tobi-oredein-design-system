# Tobi Oredein Brand System Redesign

## The Brief

**Who:** Female CEO of Black Ballad — a women's lifestyle brand at the intersection of media, data, community, and technology.

**Problem:** Current brand system is "safe editorial" (cream/beige/serif) — competent but forgettable. Doesn't communicate innovation, doesn't command boardrooms, doesn't reflect someone building the future.

**Goal:** Create a brand language that feels like the future of Black women's media — rigorous, different, and unmistakably forward-thinking.

---

## Discovery Insights

### Presence & Energy
- **Trusted guide** — wisdom walking into the room, not disruption for disruption's sake
- **Identity gap to close:** "Innovative/Future-forward" — she's building something new but it doesn't look like it

| Attribute | Direction |

## Creative Direction

### Inspiration Analysis (from provided images)

The three inspiration images reveal a consistent visual language:
- **Typography:** Heavy bold headlines + elegant script/italic sublines (the bold contrast)
- **Color mood:** Warm, rich environments — olive, terracotta, warm wood, blush, warm golds
- **Text treatment:** Warm cream/off-white text, integrated naturally with imagery
- **Energy:** Personal but polished, conversational confidence, rich layered backgrounds

### Color Palette (Refined)

**Primary:**
- Deep warm black (#1A1614) — slightly warmer than pure black
- Warm off-white (#FAF6F1) — for text overlays on imagery

**Signature colors:**
- Terracotta (#C4654A) — the bold "unexpected earth" accent
- Deep olive (#5C5C3D) — sophisticated warmth
- Warm gold (#C9A962) — for premium/metallic moments

**Supporting:**
- Blush (#E8D4D0) — soft accent, used sparingly
- Warm grey (#8A8580) — breathing room, captions

### Typography System (Refined based on inspiration)

**Display/Headlines:** Bold condensed serif with presence
- Something like Playfair Display Black, or a bold editorial serif
- Heavy weights for impact — commands attention
- ALL CAPS or Title Case depending on context

**Elegant sublines:** Script or italic serif
- Creates the "light" in bold contrast
- Sophisticated, personal, warm
- Options: Cormorant Garamond Italic (keep but reposition), or a modern script

**Body:** Clean humanist sans
- DM Sans can stay but used differently
- Or upgrade to something with more character (e.g., Satoshi, General Sans)

**Data/Technical:** Monospace for data credibility
- JetBrains Mono or similar

### Visual Language (Refined)

**Text-over-imagery style:**
- Bold headlines integrated naturally with rich photography/backgrounds
- NOT text slapped onto images — feels organic, editorial
- Warm off-white/cream text, never pure white

**Layouts:**
- Can be asymmetric but also centered for quotes/statements
- Leave breathing room — strategic maximalism, not clutter
- Text should feel like it belongs in the space

**Texture:**
- Subtle grain/noise for warmth
- Rich, layered backgrounds (like the inspiration images)
- Warm metallic accents for premium moments

**Patterns:**
- Minimal pattern use — let typography and color do the work
- If used, abstract and geometric (not Ankara — universal)

**Photography treatment:**
- Rich, warm environments
- Natural colors (no heavy filters)
- Can handle text overlay beautifully
- Think: sophisticated interiors, urban settings, personal moments
